# Uff Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 24, 2026

## 1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how Third Fork Labs LLC ("Third Fork Labs",
"we", "us") collects, uses, and discloses personal information when you
use the Uff mobile application and related services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect identifying details such as email
address and account profile information (for example, display name).

### 2.2 Activity and Fitness Information

When you use Uff to track workouts, we process data such as activity type,
timestamps, distance, duration, route points, split data, and activity photos
you upload.

### 2.3 Device and Technical Information

We process technical metadata needed to operate the service, including device
type, app version, and diagnostic telemetry.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use personal information to:

- Provide and maintain core app functionality.
- Sync your account and activity data across sessions.
- Deliver social features (for example, profile visibility and follow flows).
- Detect abuse, enforce limits, and protect service integrity.
- Improve product reliability, safety, and performance.

## 4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We may share information:

- With service providers that process data on our behalf.
- When required by law, legal process, or valid governmental request.
- To protect rights, safety, and platform security.
- As directed by your in-app privacy and sharing settings.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain account and activity information for as long as needed to provide the
service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce terms.
You can request deletion of your account subject to applicable requirements.

## 6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to
protect personal information. No system is perfectly secure.

## 7. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete,
or export personal information, and to object to or restrict certain
processing. You may also manage sharing behavior through in-app controls.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Uff is not intended for children under 13 (or a higher age threshold where
required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from
children in violation of applicable law.

## 9. International Transfers and Hosting

Personal information is processed on servers located in the United States
(AWS US East, Northern Virginia). If you access the service from outside the
United States, your information will be transferred to the United States for
processing. We apply safeguards required by applicable law for international
transfers.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be
communicated through the app or other appropriate channels.
